Puzzle Learning Cards


Here is a C# program I wrote to read in a .txt file having alternate questions and answers, one line each, and print out four pages of puzzle cards having q and a's matching when you arrange the cards correctly in a six by six pattern. I used "cover stock" 67 lb paper available from Staples. It is pretty thick, but does barely feed in the printer I have (HP Envy). Then you can laminate. Staples can do lamination. They can also copy onto some thick glossy paper that is almost like playing cards, without lamination. I've made this downloadable.

download PuzzleLearningCardsSetup.exe
